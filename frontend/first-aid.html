First Aid Guide

Burns

  • Cool under running water for 10–20 minutes.
  • Do NOT apply toothpaste or oil.
  • Cover with clean cloth.
  • Seek help if severe or blistering.

Bleeding

  • Apply firm pressure with clean cloth.
  • Elevate the injured area.
  • If bleeding doesn't stop after 10 mins, seek help.

Fainting

  • Lay person flat on their back.
  • Elevate legs slightly.
  • Ensure fresh air.
  • If unconscious > 1 minute, call emergency.

Choking

  • Encourage coughing.
  • Perform back blows if needed.
  • Call emergency services if breathing stops.
